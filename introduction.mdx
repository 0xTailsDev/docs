## Overview

Tails Exchange is a cutting-edge perpetuals trading platform built on Sonic Chain, powered by UniDex's robust aggregation technology. As the most advanced perpetuals exchange native to Sonic Chain, we offer traders unprecedented access to deep liquidity and optimal trading routes.

## What Makes Us Different

Tails Exchange stands out with three key features:

1. **Rehypothecated Market Making**: Our innovative USD.m vault system enables multi-protocol yield optimization, allowing liquidity providers to earn from market making while accessing yields across multiple DeFi protocols.

2. **Extensive Market Coverage**: Access the widest selection of trading pairs on Sonic Chain, from popular cryptocurrencies to exotic pairs unavailable elsewhere.

3. **Deep Aggregated Liquidity**: Trade with confidence using our advanced aggregation technology that sources liquidity from all major platforms on Sonic Chain.
